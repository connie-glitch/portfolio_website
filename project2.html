Open Sources

"Open Sources" is the philosophy that everything is a remix, reinterprtation, and reference to existing ideas. Embodying this concept, I designed the pages of the book to be torn and collected, giving permission for readers to literally take any content out of the book to create a mini publication of the ideas and texts they want to take away.

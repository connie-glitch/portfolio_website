Chinroserie Grotesk

A project originated from researching patterns and porcelain objects from chinroserie, the Western imitation of Chinese ornamental styles. This design movement popularized racialized depction of 'Oriental' features and bodies. Responding to this, I created a pattern with open source p5js code that exaggerates this existing precedent, in which the body parts of the 'oriental' woman literally fuses with ornaments to create grotesque forms. Rather than allowing the viewer to passivly admire the ornament, the pattern confronts them with the colonial implications of chinroseire.
